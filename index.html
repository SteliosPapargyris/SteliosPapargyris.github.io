Precipiteau

A real-time European rainfall nowcasting platform covering 35+ countries. Fuses EUMETNET OPERA radar composites, DWD ICON-EU NWP model output, and H-SAF satellite precipitation (MSG/SEVIRI) into a unified interactive map, updated every 15 minutes around the clock.

Implements the Ramer ice-fraction algorithm for precipitation type diagnosis (rain, snow, freezing rain, ice pellets, hail) at 1 km terrain resolution, and generates 1-hour nowcasts via PySTEPS optical-flow.
